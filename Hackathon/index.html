About Us

"Bits 2 Bytes" is a technical fest held every year by Bengal Institute of Technology, in the college campus. It lats for two days.Bits 2 Bytes emphasises on showcasing the enthusiastic ideas and innovations in students.
There are many events held in the fest. Topics from all aspects like robotics, computing, gaming, designing,film making,etc.
For years Bits 2 Bytes has been successfully showcasing the astonishing ideas by the students of BIT. Besides all theses, there are cash prizes for the winning projects.
